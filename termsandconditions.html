Welcome to Caring Hands Midwife

Thank you for using my Complementary Therapy Service for pregnancy and postnatal, provided by Caring Hands Midwife.

 

By using my services you are agreeing to these terms and conditions.  Please read these terms and conditions carefully before undertaking any complementary treatment offered by Caring Hands Midwife.  Additional terms may apply to relevant specific treatments and following discussion at consultation those additional terms become part of your agreement with me if you purchase these treatments.

 

Privacy Agreement

I am committed to protecting your privacy and deliver services with confidentiality and maintain secure records in the course of my treatments in accordance with GDPR, insurance and NMC requirements.

 

Purchases

If you purchase services from Caring Hands Midwife you will be asked to supply information relevant to the treatment, without limitation, on your current health, medications, pregnancy and obstetric history at your first consultation and prior to commencement of treatment.

By purchasing a course of treatments you agree to the terms of the length of the course to receive the full benefits of treatment.  Courses not completed may result in therapeutic benefits not being achieved.

Infection Control and Covid 19

Caring Hands Midwife has your safety and wellbeing at the heart of her treatments.  To maintain your safety a Covid 19 Risk assessment will be completed prior to any treatments.  Treatments will be spaced with 30 minutes between clients to minimise contact with other clients, clean the treatment area and ventilate the treatment room.

Aftercare

Treatments can result in a healing reaction which can present as physical or emotional.  A healing reaction should not last any longer than 24-36hours post treatment and if symptoms persist advice from a GP, Midwife or Obstetrician should be sought. Clients are advised to

  • Drink plenty of water
  • Avoid drinking tea coffee or alcohol
  • Avoid strenuous exercise
  • Rest as much as possible for a few hours post treatment.
  • Record any reactions and report back at subsequent appointments

 

Payment

Payment for services will be required in advance prior to confirmation of appointments.  An invoice will be issued followed by receipt of payment.

 

Cancellation Policy

A minimum of 24hrs notice is required.  Such notice may be given in person, by telephone, email, text message or by any other means.  I reserve the right to charge a cancellation fee of 20% to cover administration costs.  In the event of hospitalisation preventing attendance treatment can be deferred at no additional cost.

 

If Late for Treatment

Clients arriving late for treatment may result in shorter treatments to prevent impact to the client attending the next appointment.

 

Complaints Procedure

Feedback on services will be welcomed with any dissatisfaction acknowledged and addressed to improve services.  Complaints will be managed promptly within 2 weeks of receipt.

 

Professional Behaviour

Mutual respect and courteous conduct will be a basic expectation of both parties.  Client confidentiality will be in accordance with good practice and the NMC Code.

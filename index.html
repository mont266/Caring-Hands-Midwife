About Us

Hi and welcome to Caring Hands Midwife. My name is Barbara Strawbridge and I live in Coleraine in the beautiful North Coast of Northern Ireland. I have more than 36 years experience working in the health service as a qualified nurse, health visitor and midwife and currently registered with the Nursing and Midwifery Council.

I have always had an interest in complementary therapies and as an experienced midwife, with pregnant women at the heart of everything I do, want to provide women with an alternative option for treatment and management of common pregnancy issues, conditions and ailments in pregnancy through use of complementary therapies alongside their existing NHS care. Not only does my service compliment standard maternity care, treatments are evidence-based holistic treatments which provide additional time with me as a registered midwife who can listen to your pregnancy worries and concerns and answer your questions in a relaxed calm private space up to 8 weeks following the birth of your baby. I also have the experience of complementary therapy treatments such as massage and reflexology to the general public and can bring some of that experience and skill along with the knowledge, skill and experience as a midwife to help you experience a positive pregnancy, birth and early post-natal journey.

I have completed my Diploma in Midwifery Complementary Therapies through Dr Denise Tiran’s Expectancy programme in London and can provide a range of services to pregnant women facilitating a holistic approach to pregnancy and birth. Expectancy is the leading authority on Maternity Complementary Therapies and I was delighted to be awarded runner up to "Best Student of the Year" in 2022 for my work.

I am also currently undertaking a Certificate in Midwifery Acupuncture with Expectancy and will be available to offer acupuncture treatments from early 2023.

I am currently insured with FHT, FEDANT and Balens and covered by the Royal College of Midwifery. I am also a member of Expectancy’s Licensed Consultancy Programme.

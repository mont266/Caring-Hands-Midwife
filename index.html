About Us

Hi and welcome to Caring Hands Midwife. My name is Barbara Strawbridge and I live in Coleraine in the beautiful North Coast of Northern Ireland. I have more than 36 years experience working in the health service as a qualified nurse, health visitor and midwife and currently registered with the Nursing and Midwifery Council.

I have always had an interest in complementary therapies and currently provide services in reflexology and massage to the generic population. However as an experienced midwife, with pregnant women at the heart of everything I do I wanted to provide pregnant women with an alternative option for treatment and management of common issues and conditions in pregnancy through complementary therapies which could compliment their existing NHS care.

I have completed my Diploma in Complementary Therapies in Midwifery through Dr Denise Tiran’s Expectancy programme in London and can provide a range of services to pregnant women facilitating a holistic approach to pregnancy and birth. Expectancy is the leading authority on Maternity Complementary Therapies and I was delighted to be awarded runner up to Best Student of the year in 2022 for my work.

I am also currently undertaking a Certificate in Acupuncture in Midwifery with Expectancy and hope to be able to offer acupuncture for treatments early 2023.

I am currently insured with FHT, FEDANT and Balens and covered by the Royal College of Midwifery. I am also a member of Expectancy’s Licensed Consultancy Programme.
